West Village

Your dream house - closer than you'd imagine

A place near you

West village residence is located near the thriving heart of Bulgaria - only a 30 minute drive from Sofia! Located in the eastern end of Slivnitsa, the housing complex provides a rural, relaxing environment, combined with the convenience of easy travel to the biggest metropolis in Bulgaria.
